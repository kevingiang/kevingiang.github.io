Services

Offering interior steam cleaning, exterior surface cleaning, ceramic coating application, hydrophobic glass coating application, and regular maintenance packages all at your home.

Interior Steam Clean and Protection Package

  • Carpet floor mats and interior carpeting are thoroughly vacuumed, steam cleaned, shampooed with fabric-specific cleaner, and thoroughly extracted
  • Cloth/leather seats are vacuumed and steam cleaned
  • Leather seats are lightly dressed with a high quality restorer
  • Door panels are deep cleaned with Gtechniq I2 Tri-Clean, an antimicrobial disinfectant, and rinsed with dry steam
  • Interior glass is degreased with streak-free alcohol solution
  • All interior plastics are degreased and disinfected with a castile soap and Gtechniq I2 Triclean, and dressed with Carpro PERL to restore color and protect from UV damage

Optional Services

  • Trioxygen treatment to eliminate odors and airborne microbes
  • Leatherique two-part treatment for deep, ultra-luxurious leather nourishment
