Services

Offering interior steam cleaning, exterior surface cleaning, ceramic coating application, hydrophobic glass coating application, and regular maintenance packages all at your home.

Interior Steam Clean and Protection Package

  • Carpet floor mats and interior carpeting are thoroughly vacuumed, steam cleaned, shampooed with fabric-specific cleaner, and thoroughly extracted
  • Cloth/leather seats are vacuumed and steam cleaned
  • Leather seats are lightly dressed with a high quality restorer
  • Door panels are deep cleaned with Gtechniq I2 Tri-Clean, an antimicrobial disinfectant, and rinsed with dry steam
  • Interior glass is degreased with streak-free alcohol solution
  • All interior plastics are degreased and disinfected with a castile soap and Gtechniq I2 Triclean, and dressed with Carpro PERL to restore color and protect from UV damage

Optional Services

  • Trioxygen treatment to eliminate odors and airborne microbes
  • Leatherique two-part treatment for deep, ultra-luxurious leather nourishment

Exterior Facelift and Protection Package

  • All exterior surfaces are treated to a high-pressure rinse and a rich blanket of high-sudsing snow foam through an MTM Hydro Parts PF22.2 Foam Cannon and left to dwell to soften dirt
  • After leaving to soak, the paintwork is gently washed with an ultra-soft Cyclone microfiber mitt and rinsed
  • Deeply embedded contaminates are neutralized with Gtechniq W6 Iron and Fallout Remover and rinsed
  • Exterior is re-foamed and then mechanically decontaminated with clay to gently exfoliate paint
  • A light machine polish is applied to increase gloss, remove light scratches, and prime surface for wax
  • Ultra-durable wax is meticulously applied by hand to seal freshly exfoliated paintwork and increase gloss

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coatings are ~90% preparation work and require meticulous attention during each step. For best results, the paint is corrected one 16-inch square section at a time, hence the jump in price between the different vehicle sizes.

Silver Ceramic Package

  • One-stage machine polish to remove most defects
  • One coating of Gtechniq Crystal Serum Light
